## Five Phase Contract

- Discovery: scan real signals and state.
- Handoff: select one opportunity or report no action.
- Verification: verify findings against source evidence.
- Persistence: update state and append run log.
- Scheduling: stop, schedule, pause, or escalate.

## Human Gates

- No source changes in L1.
- Escalate pricing, payment, auth, secrets, legal, compliance, or major product direction.
