## Five Phase Contract

- Discovery: inspect route/flow and collect evidence.
- Handoff: choose one bounded intervention with hypothesis and rollback.
- Verification: run browser checks and relevant tests.
- Persistence: update state, failed attempts, and run log.
- Scheduling: continue only if a verified follow-up is cheap and safe.

## Human Gates

- Require approval for major navigation, auth, payment, pricing, brand-sensitive copy, or irreversible changes.
